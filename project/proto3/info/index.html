What is bullying?

There are different types of bullying but the things they all have in common are:

  • imbalance of power
  • intent to cause harm
  • repetition

Bullying is commonly seen as put downs, attacks, rude comments, harmful language, or cyber aggression. Bullying is a universal way to describe someone intentionally making another person feel bad or less than the bully.

What can I do if I'm being bullied?

Follow these steps if you're being bullied:

  • speak up against bullying by being firm and saying something like "stop it"
  • walk away
  • tell an adult you trust
  • report it through this site
  • stick together and stay with a friend or group who has your back

What do I do if I see bullying?

Do not join in, bullies love attention. Here are some things you can do to help:

  • stop any rumors that come from it by explaining that it's a toxic situation and shouldn't be spread to others
  • stand up to the bully by calmly telling them to stop being hurtful to others
  • tell an adult like the counselor or a teacher
  • encourage the bullied person to talk to an adult or report the incident
  • report it yourslef through Build Up or ask the bullied person to
  • offer support to the bullied person

How can I avoid being bullied?

Bullies can be scary but know that you're not alone. Try these tips to avoid being bullied:

  • don't give the bullies a chance, avoid them or specific routes to school or class so you don't put yourself in a bad position
  • avoid unsupervised areas of school
  • sit near the front of the bus by the driver
  • find a buddy and stick together
  • stand tall and be brave, don't show them it gets to you

What are the warning signs of bullying?

There are warning signs for those being bullied and also those bullying otehers.

Being bullied:

  • reluctant to go to school or certain places
  • silent about how school is
  • lost or damaged belongings often
  • academic problems
  • low self-esteem
  • difficulty concentrating
  • social isolation
  • quiet, depressed, irritable, anxious

Bullying others:

  • physical or verbal fighting
  • enjoys putting others down
  • has new belongings that cannot be explained
  • disprespectful and disregards rules
  • bad attitude
  • enjoys violence
  • quick to blame others
  • superiority complex

What can I do if a friend/family member/significant other is bullying me?

If you feel bullied by people who are supposed to be close and kind to you, try these suggestions:

  • ask them to please stop and explain how it makes you feel
  • talk to a counselor at school about it
  • tell your parents or an adult
  • open up about it to someone you trust
