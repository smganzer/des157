What is bullying?

There are different types of bullying but the things they all have in common are:

  • imbalance of power
  • intent to cause harm
  • repetition

Bullying is commonly seen as put downs, attacks, rude comments, harmful language, or cyber aggression. Bullying is a universal way to describe someone intentionally making another person feel bad or less than the bully.
